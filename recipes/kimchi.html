Kimchi recipe

Description

Kimichi - a traditional korean dish, made from salted and fermentized napa cabbage. One of the most popular salad in the world. In addition, one of the most popular Korean dishes, and ingridient for various soups, ramens and entrees. Today, you will learn how to make this great dish.

Ingridients

  • Salt
  • Napa cabbage
  • Fermented shrimps
  • Chili flakes
  • Sweet rice flour
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Raddish
  • Carrot
  • Green Onion

Recipe

  1. Rub salt into the cut napa cabbage, and leave it to soak in the water for 2 hours
  2. Leave it to rest for 2 hours, turning it every 30 min
  3. Clean cabbage under cold water
  4. In small bowl combine water and sweet rice flour
  5. Cook the mixture on the medium heat for about 10 minutes untill it bubbles
  6. Add some sugar and cook for another minute
  7. Pour cooled porridge in the bowl with garlic, ginger, onion, fish sauce, fermented salted shrimps, chili flakes
  8. Additionally, add radish, carrot, and green onions
  9. Mix napa cabbage with the mixture of sauce and vegetables
  10. Leave it to ferment
