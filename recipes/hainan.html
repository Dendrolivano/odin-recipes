Hainan Chocken Rice recipe

Description

Hainanese Chicken Rice is a dish that makes you feel both pleased and warmed with every bite. It's a culinary masterpiece where perfectly poached chicken, with its silky, firm, and tender texture, takes center stage. But the true star is the fragrant rice, cooked in rich chicken fat and broth, which gives it an aromatic and savory flavor that stands on its own. This incredible meal is completed by a trio of dipping sauces—a zesty ginger-scallion oil, a vibrant chili sauce, and a savory soy dressing—that perfectly complement the tender chicken and flavorful rice. It's a dish that's simple at its heart but delivers a truly luxurious and deeply satisfying experience.

Ingridients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Scallions
  • Ginger
  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Dark soy sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken thighs under the water and cut excessive fat.
  2. Add scallions and ginger into the deep bowl, then add the thighs into the same bowl.
  3. Cook in the simmering water for nearly 35 minutes.
  4. Cool the chicken down in the ice bath.
  5. In the deep skillet, or wok, add oil with minced garlic, then add three cups of jasmine rice.
  6. Put the rice in the bowl of rice cooker and add chicken stock.
  7. For the sauce put 4 chili peppers into the food processor untill it's porridge like
  8. The resulted chili porridge add to the normal suyce sauce, along with minced garlic and green onions
  9. When chicken thighs cooled down cut it, add chicken stock rice along it, and put some chili soy sauce on the chicken.
